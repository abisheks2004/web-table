DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department is dedicated to equipping students with the skills and knowledge required to excel in their careers. With a strong emphasis on hands-on learning, industry collaborations, and innovative problem-solving, the department offers undergraduate and graduate degree programs that cater to the needs of the tech industry. Students can choose from a range of specializations, including software engineering, data science, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity, to name a few. The CSE department also provides numerous career-oriented opportunities, such as internships, research projects, and networking events, to help students launch their careers and stay ahead in the competitive job market.
